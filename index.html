About Me

I'm Joshua Krutz, but you can call me Josh.

I grew up in Laurel, Maryland which is rightfully positioned between DC and Baltimore. I graduated from Reservoir High School in 2018 and I currently attend The United States Air Force Academy in Colorado Springs. I will graduate in May of 2022, and I coulnd't be more excited! At the Academy, I am studying computer science and cyber science. The projects and courses that typically pique my interest have some degree of machine learning. I have two AI classes under my belt. The first course introduced me to searching algorithms, reinforcement learning, and classifiers. The second course was more technical and I was able to apply AI concepts to an autonomous system that searched and delivered supplies to a "deployed unit" in need. My current goal is to get into a graduate studies program to continue my machine learning journey.

Beyond the academic environment, I like to unwind with friends in the mountains, snowboard, read futuristic sci-fi books, and visit my favorite chain restaurant Torchy's Tacos! I also really enjoy art, photography, and design. Actually, every icon, picture, or drawing you see on this website was handcrafted by me! If the blue light from my monitor doesn't dissuade me too much, I love to game and pursue CS passion projects. At the moment, Minecraft and Jedi Fallen Order have won my heart for best videogames and I am writing a shape classifier for a videogame I am developing with two other cadets at the Academy.
